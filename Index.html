Econauts

Masters of sustainable living

Econauts are Individuals who want to live sustainably. No matter the climate, Econauts bravely learn about the land and present resources. Studying the enviornment, Econauts figure out the best way to co-exist with the plants, animals and most importantly, your fellow Econauts.
